Cassava Leaf

Decription

This is an indegineous dish from west africa easy to prepare

Ingredients

  • Ground cassava leaves
  • Peanut butter
  • Okra
  • Pepper and onions
  • Seasonings
  • Palm oil
  • Meat/fish/chicken

Steps

  1. Cook the cassava leaves for about 20 minutes on medium heat
  2. Add 3 spoons of peanut butter and let it boil for 10 minutes
  3. Blend the oinions and pepper and add it
  4. Grind the okra and add it
  5. Meaure a cup of oil/palmoil and add to it
  6. Add meat/fish/chicken if you use any
