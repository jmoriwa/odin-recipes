Beef stew

Decription

Beef stew is a classic dinner staple in so many households around the world. There are soup and stew adaptations of beef stew. This is one of them.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Pepper
  • Beef
  • Pepper and onions
  • Seasonings
  • Olive oil
  • Potatoes

Steps

  1. Combine flour, garlic powder and salt & pepper. Toss beef in flour mixture.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large Dutch oven or pot. Cook the beef and onions until browned.
  3. Add seasonings
  4. Add 2 cups of water to soften onions
  5. Add potatoes
